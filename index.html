Chao Pinhole Surgical Technique

The Chao Pinhole Surgical Technique is an alternative treatment to receding gums that is faster, not nearly as painful, and can be a long-term solution to dealing with receding gums. Gingival recession is the technical term for when gum tissue deteriorates and exposes more of the tooth. This not only robs you of a beautiful smile but the more the gums recede, the higher the likelihood is that the tooth’s root will be exposed. This creates pain, sensitivity and discomfort.
